About Me

I am a Computer Engineering BS/MS major with a minor in Economics at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) looking for co-op and internship opportunities to achieve expertise in fields of computer engineering. The bond between hardware and software has always intrigued me. The ability of writing a few lines of code and making things physically move, to this day still gives me that "WOW" feeling. Several of my projects, goals, and interests revolve around both software and hardware skills. My main interest lies in robotic engineering, with the goal to one day be working on the front lines of Surgical Robotic Systems. Robotic surgery is the next step in medical advancement. The potential it has to help patients could change the way people get treated in a way never imagined. The opportunity to change the long standing tradition of the medical field is why I want to be a leader in this field. My other fields of experience and interests include Electronics, Mobile Development, Web Applications, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. As a student, I strive to learn all that I can and understand the concepts as well as practical applications through personal projects. I have a strong base in both software and hardware. I am a fast learner and I am excited to learn new things. Being a well-rounded, full stack engineer is something I pride myself on. Also, I have and currently hold several leadership positions on campus which lead to my passion for leadership, public speaking, and making positive changes for my constituents.
