As a research assistant in the machine intelligence lab, my main tasks revolve around the Machine Intelligence Laboratory Personal Electronic Transporter (MILPET), which is an autonomous wheel chair. This wheel chair serves as the base for several research projects in the fields of computer vision, natural language processing, gesture recognition, localization, and control systems. My responsibilities include upgrading, and maintaining the software and hardware of the system. This encompasses sensor integration, obstacle avoidance algorithms, wireless control, and failure-safe circuitry.
